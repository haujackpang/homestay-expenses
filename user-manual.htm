常见问题 FAQ
Q: AI 无法识别我的单据怎么办？
AI can't read my receipt?
A: 点击 "↻ Re-scan" 重新上传更清晰的照片，或点击 "fill in manually" 手动填写。
Tap "↻ Re-scan" to upload a clearer photo, or fill in manually.
Q: 我的申请已 Submitted，还能修改吗？
Can I edit a Submitted claim?
A: 可以，在详情页点击 "Edit →"，重新修改后会自动重新提交。
Yes, tap "Edit →" in claim detail. It will re-submit after saving.
Q: 申请被拒绝后怎么办？
My claim was rejected. What now?
A: 查看拒绝原因，联系经理解决，或重新提交新申请。
Read the rejection reason and contact your manager, or submit a new claim.
Q: 经理看不到 "Manage Users" 按钮？
Manager can't see "Manage Users"?
A: "Manage Users" 只有 Admin 角色才能看到。Manager 无法管理用户账号。
"Manage Users" is only visible to Admin role accounts.
